1. Project Overview

The Better To Best Research Hub is an open-access initiative comprising 14 peer-reviewable papers. The research focuses on the structural elimination of poverty through integrated economic and civic architectures. It proposes a "Compassionate Meritocracy" that secures basic human needs while incentivizing high-value productivity and community contribution.

2. Core Economic Architectures

Creative Currency Octaves (CCO)

CCO is a dual-currency system designed for post-scarcity and automation-driven economies.

  • Basic Units (BU): An opt-in currency for essentials (food, housing, utilities) that expires monthly to prevent hoarding and ensure circulation.
  • Primary Currency: Used for discretionary spending and long-term wealth.
  • Conversion Multipliers: Businesses and creators can convert BU into primary currency at rates ranging from 1x to 9x, depending on their "Octave Level."
  • The Phi-Rate: A qualitative multiplier (~1.618x) applied to work recognized for exceptional beauty, utility, or social harmony.

Public Trust Foundations (PTF) & Housing (PTH)

These entities provide the physical and commercial stability required for the CCO system to function.

  • Public Trust Foundations: Not-for-profit community businesses that provide foundational goods.
  • Public Trust Housing: A model for community-owned housing where residents hold collective equity and exercise democratic control.

3. Civic & Governance Frameworks

Citizens Internet Portal (CIP)

The digital infrastructure for the entire ecosystem. It handles:

  • Secure management of CCO basic units and primary currency.
  • Transparent democratic voting for PTH/PTF management.
  • Direct communication between citizens and regional planners.

Social Zone Harmonization (SZH)

A framework for regional organization that allows communities to self-sort based on lifestyle preferences (e.g., family-focused, wellness-focused, or high-creativity zones) while maintaining overarching democratic coordination.

4. Key Research Papers

The hub includes specialized analysis on:

  1. Optimal Transfer Design: Mathematics of the 1x–9x multiplier system.
  2. Dual-Currency Inflation: Stability analysis of expiring vs. non-expiring currency.
  3. Simulation Analysis: Monte Carlo and agent-based modeling of the CCO-PTF framework.
  4. Real Estate Transformation: Strategies for transitioning to Public Trust Housing.
