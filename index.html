Creative Currency Octaves (CCO)

Revolutionary dual-tier incentive systems that combine octave-based capacity limits with personal multiplier rates to eliminate welfare cliffs while enhancing work incentives. Basic units distributed universally for essentials, with conversion opportunities through productive activities.
