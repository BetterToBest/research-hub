Economic Framework Papers

Core research on Creative Currency Octaves (CCO) and dual-currency systems for sustainable economic transformation.

  • Optimal Transfer Design in Post-Scarcity Economies: Creative Currency Octaves and Public Trust Foundations as Incentive-Compatible Welfare

    Introduces a dual-tier incentive system combining octave-based capacity limits and personal multiplier rates (1x to 14x+). Resolves the equity-efficiency tradeoff by creating productive conversion opportunities.

    PDF Version

  • Dual Currency Systems and Inflation: CCO's Price Stability Mechanisms

    Examines inflation dynamics in CCO systems, demonstrating stable equilibrium through sectoral restrictions and conversion limits. Provides mathematical models for preventing monetary disruption.

    PDF Version

  • Economic Modeling and Simulation Analysis of Creative Currency Octaves

    Comprehensive simulation modeling of CCO system dynamics with agent-based models demonstrating stability across various economic scenarios and implementation pathways.

    PDF Version

  • Creative Currency Octaves and Public Trust Foundations: Integrated Framework

    Establishes theoretical foundation for combined CCO-PTF systems, demonstrating synergistic effects between currency innovation and collective ownership models.

    PDF Version

Implementation & Applications

Practical frameworks for real-world deployment of CCO and Public Trust Housing (PTH) systems across various sectors.

  • Transforming the US Real Estate Market Through Public Trust Housing

    Introduces "Acre Equity" mechanism for collective wealth building. Models show PTH achieving 15-20% market penetration within a decade with $70,000 average wealth accumulation per household.

    PDF Version

  • Cultural Value Integration in Economic Systems: The CCO Framework

    The CCO Framework as the first currency system explicitly backed by "publicly-endowed art and creation," with multi-tier conversion rates and the Phi rate (1.618x) for productive beauty.

    PDF Version

  • Community-Based Monetary Innovation for Development

    Development economics framework achieving poverty reduction below 5% within 15 years. Superior ROI of $1,847 per beneficiary versus traditional aid approaches in emerging economies.

    PDF Version

Financing & Scaling Strategies

Financial mechanisms and scaling approaches for systematic deployment of post-scarcity economic systems.

  • PTH Independent Financing Models: From Grassroots Cooperation to Philanthropic Scale

    Examines government-independent financing models for PTH implementation, including the "avalanche method" for collective mortgage payoff achieving $85,000 in interest savings. Financial modeling demonstrates PTH sustainability with just 30-40 pay-in households.

    PDF Version

  • PTH Government Integration: Scaling Through Public Investment

    Analysis of government investment scenarios for PTH implementation, from pilot programs ($50B) to revolutionary transformation ($500B). Financial architecture demonstrating 50% market penetration achievable within 5-19 years.

    PDF Version

  • Universal Implementation Framework for Human Thriving: CCO-PTF-CIP-SZH

    Comprehensive guide for implementing all four systems together, with technology specifications, legal frameworks, and scaling strategies for achieving post-scarcity economic conditions.

    PDF Version
